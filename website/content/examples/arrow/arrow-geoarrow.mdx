Mixed geometry rendering from one GeoArrow-style DenseUnion column. Point rows are
rendered with `ArrowPointRenderer`, LineString and MultiLineString rows with
`ArrowLineRenderer`, and Polygon and MultiPolygon rows with `ArrowPolygonRenderer`.
